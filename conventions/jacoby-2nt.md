# Jacoby 2NT

## When to use

By responder, shows 13pts with a 4 cards fit support in majors.

## Responses (by opener)

```
M := opener's major
Z := Any suit except M
```

- 3M means extra values
- 3Z means a splinter
- 4Z means a long and strong new suit
- 3NT shows extra and is better than 3M (extra extra wow)
